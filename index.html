Bonus Pints is a craft beer & wine spin on the classic arcade! Tons of FREE to play & coin play arcade games with a focus on craft beer & wine!

While we may hang our hat on offering craft beers and wine to patrons while enjoying our games; Turns out we are also open to all ages.

If that wasn't enough we share the space with THE RECORD FARM! Which is Cass County's only independent record store!
